CONCEPT

The life'n'movie project is a complete training that through a cinematic approach serves to better handle corporate interactions.
It is a method for actual staff members of a company
to act out daily business situations.

THE COMPANY BECOME A MOVIE SET
TO BE REBORN IN THE COMPANY TO MAKE THE COMPANY REBORN

We create a movie set company to create a new approach to rise the challenges posed by the new global market.
Movie set Set
team Script
Improvisation acting
Acting concepts
Study of the character = know yourself + interpretation

SUBJECTS

► ACTION
► ACTING
► SET DESIGN
► SCRIPT

Trough a film setting created with props belonging to daily business life, you can stage relational issues and conflicts as authentic as possible. The sceneriers will become a tool for symbolic action.

This innovative coaching approach helps facing daily issues, as anxiety, fear, stress, rigidity of role, frustration, conflict, distrust, indecision, in the most authentic fun way, as result. Trough by filming the acted out interactions, a very detailed video analyses from different angles is possible. To learn to reprogram oneself and to give new meaning to:

► TIME
► QUALITY
► INNOVATION
► INTUITION
► CREATIVITY

Improving staff motivation by self awareness. This cinematic training gives you new abilities to operate on both the personal and the company’s welfare.

GOALS

Before filming any acting a particular attention will be given to define objectives. This prefilming preparations consist of 5 steps:

► Abandonment of the old schemes and immobility
► Goals and identity
► Ability to chose
► Skills and inner strength
► Processing capacity
► Bungee jumping emotional risk
► Creativity and reconstruction
► Self confrontation

► Focus on your goals
► Describe your goal with simplicity and clarity
► Lineup your goal to the company’s objectives and values
► Visualise your goal as already achived
► Express your goal with belief, motivation, and optimism

Certain of our own identity, ready for transformation the acting starts! The participants will have the possibility to break oneself down and build oneself up again with observed findings. The scenic space will allow participants to travel with their imagination, passing trough eight primary steps.

The filmed interactions provide transparency and help the participants to be more self aware of correct behaviour in the company.

ASPIRATIONS

Life'n'movie wants to be a new intuitive and revolutionary method to deal with the transformation and new challenges that the world of work produces every day: a simple and authentic training that through an artistic and creative form and the organization set method face important business themes such as professional argue and emphatic leadership, harmonic team work and motivational co-working. Ultimately the concept results from different angles wich provide each participant with a better understanding of their social dynamics.
